# Welcome to MindHelper — Student Mental Health & Productivity Assistant

## What is MindHelper?

MindHelper is an AI-powered chatbot designed specifically for university students at Asia Pacific University (APU). It provides evidence-based guidance on mental health, academic productivity, stress management, and study skills.

MindHelper is available 24/7, completely free, and confidential. It is here to support you — not to judge you.

---

## What MindHelper Can Help You With

### 1. Mood Check-In
Start any conversation by telling MindHelper how you feel. Rate your mood from 1–10 or describe it in words. MindHelper will respond with appropriate support, coping strategies, or motivation based on where you are emotionally.

Examples:
- "I feel really stressed today"
- "My mood is 3/10 — I'm exhausted"
- "I'm feeling good and ready to study!"

### 2. Study Techniques
Ask for evidence-based study methods tailored to your situation. MindHelper knows:
- The Pomodoro Technique (timed focus sessions)
- Active Recall and Spaced Repetition
- The Feynman Technique
- Cornell Notes and Mind Mapping
- How to use Anki and other study tools

Examples:
- "What's the best way to study for my exam?"
- "Explain the Pomodoro Technique"
- "I keep forgetting what I study — help"

### 3. Daily Study Planner
Tell MindHelper how much time you have and what you need to study, and it will create a personalised Pomodoro-based study plan for your day.

Examples:
- "Give me a study plan for today — I have 4 hours and an essay due Friday"
- "Plan my exam week"
- "Help me schedule tomorrow"

### 4. Stress & Burnout Relief
Get immediate, practical stress relief techniques including breathing exercises, grounding techniques, and longer-term burnout recovery strategies.

Examples:
- "I'm overwhelmed — help me calm down"
- "Teach me a breathing exercise"
- "I think I'm burnt out. What do I do?"

### 5. Habit Tracker Guidance
Learn how to build and maintain positive academic habits using habit stacking, implementation intentions, and evidence-based tracking methods.

Examples:
- "How do I build a consistent study habit?"
- "Help me create a morning routine"
- "What habits should I track as a student?"

### 6. Assignment Help
Break down complex assignments, overcome procrastination, manage multiple deadlines, and get guidance on structure, research, and referencing.

Examples:
- "I don't know how to start my assignment"
- "Help me plan my report structure"
- "I have three assignments due this week — help me prioritise"

### 7. Mental Health Support
Get compassionate, non-judgemental guidance on anxiety, stress, imposter syndrome, sleep problems, burnout, and more. MindHelper provides psychoeducation and connects you to professional resources when needed.

Examples:
- "I feel like I don't belong here"
- "I'm always anxious — is this normal?"
- "I can't sleep before exams"

## Important: MindHelper Is Not a Medical Professional

MindHelper provides general guidance and educational information. It is NOT a substitute for professional mental health care.

If you are experiencing:
- Persistent depression, anxiety, or hopelessness
- Thoughts of self-harm or suicide
- Difficulties that are significantly affecting your daily life

Please contact:
- **APU Student Counselling Unit**: Available through the APU student portal — free and confidential for all APU students
- **Befrienders Malaysia (Crisis Line)**: 03-7627 2929 — available 24 hours, free, completely confidential
- **MIASA**: 03-2780 6803
- **Malaysia MOH Mental Health Helpline**: 03-2935 9935

## How to Get the Best from MindHelper

1. **Be specific** — "I have an exam on Thursday and don't know where to start" gets a better response than "help me study."
2. **Share your mood first** — MindHelper tailors its support based on how you're feeling.
3. **Ask follow-up questions** — if a response doesn't quite fit, say "can you give me something simpler?" or "what if I only have 1 hour?"
4. **Use the planners** — the daily study plan feature is particularly useful during high-stress periods.
5. **Check in regularly** — regular mood check-ins help you spot patterns in your wellbeing over time.

## About MindHelper

MindHelper was built using the Botpress Agent Development Kit (ADK) with a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture. This means every response is grounded in a curated knowledge base of evidence-based student wellbeing and productivity resources, ensuring accurate and relevant guidance.

MindHelper is a student project created at APU for the Introduction to Artificial Intelligence course. It demonstrates the application of AI knowledge representation, natural language processing, and conversational AI design in a real-world student wellbeing context.
